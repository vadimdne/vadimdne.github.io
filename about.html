About Cheese Wheels Apps

Who we are

We're a couple of developers who kicked off this journey back in 2015. Since then, we’ve been creating simple, useful apps that we actually enjoy using ourselves. Our philosophy is pretty straightforward: keep things clean, focus on quality, and really listen to the people using our apps.

Why Cheese Wheels?

The name? That’s a nod to a game our kids made up — whenever someone spots a yellow car, they shout, “Cheese wheels no punch back!” and try to be the first to tag the others. It stuck, and it just felt right. That playful spirit is something we try to bring into our work, too.
